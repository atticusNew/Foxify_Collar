  ALTERNATIVE STRUCTURES WORTH EXPLORING:

  1. REGIME-DYNAMIC: Charge calm price 60% of the time, normal 30%, pause 10%
     → Weighted avg premium is much lower than flat pricing
     → Trader sees fair prices in calm markets, accepts higher in vol markets

  2. SHORT ROLLING PROTECTION: 2-3 day renewables instead of 7-day
     → Lower per-period cost, trader pays only when they want coverage
     → Platform buys cheaper short-dated options

  3. PREMIUM REBATE: Return 20-30% of premium if protection expires unused
     → Increases trader perceived value without affecting triggered-trade economics
     → Cost: rebate_rate × (1 - trigger_rate) × premium

  4. PUT SPREAD HEDGE (tested above): Buy put spread instead of naked put
     → Reduces hedge cost significantly
     → Caps recovery but allows much lower premiums

  5. TIERED COVERAGE: Offer 50/70/100% payout levels at different prices
     → 70% coverage at 2% SL: payout = $140/10k instead of $200, premium drops ~30%

  6. LOSS-LEADER 10%: Price 10% SL at near-cost to attract users
     → Outstanding trader value (14×+ return on breach)
     → Upsell to tighter SL tiers once they're familiar with the product
