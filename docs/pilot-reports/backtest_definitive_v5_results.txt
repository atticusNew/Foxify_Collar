  WHAT WE ARE MODERATELY CONFIDENT ABOUT (MEDIUM — 60-80%):

  ~ 2% SL profitability in STRESS — tight margins, high trigger rate
  ~ Bullish mainnet pricing — estimated 1.2-2.0× Deribit, but unverified
  ~ IV/RV relationship holding in future — regime shifts could change the ratio

  WHAT WE ARE LESS CONFIDENT ABOUT (LOW — 30-50%):

  - Deep OTM put pricing (5%, 10%) — Deribit minimum tick creates a floor
    that makes BS model comparison misleading. Live $0.20-0.90/1k vs model $0.00-0.18/1k
  - Stress regime hedge costs — we have no live Deribit data from a 80% vol period
    right now. Costs could be 3-5× calm levels, reducing margins significantly
  - Execution quality — paper trading vs live fills; slippage could add 10-30%
  - Regime persistence in crypto — BTC can gap from calm to stress overnight
    (though our 2-day tenor mitigates this substantially)
