  SECTION 2: DERIBIT DVOL — THE OFFICIAL BTC VOLATILITY INDEX
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  The Deribit DVOL is to Bitcoin what the VIX is to the S&P 500.
  It's calculated from live BTC options prices on Deribit and represents
  the market's 30-day forward-looking implied volatility.

  Why DVOL is the right benchmark:
  • Published by Deribit — our primary hedge venue
  • Derived from options prices — directly reflects our hedge costs
  • Forward-looking — captures what the market EXPECTS, not what happened
  • Real-time — updates continuously, no lag
  • Industry standard — used by every institutional crypto desk
  • Publicly auditable — anyone can verify the number

  DVOL historical data not available. Using realized vol percentiles only.

  REGIME DEFINITION (Realized Vol Based):
